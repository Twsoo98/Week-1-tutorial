2017 Southeast Asian Games

The 2017 Southeast Asian Games (Malay: Sukan Asia Tenggara 2017), officially known as the 29th Southeast Asian Gamesand commonly known as Kuala Lumpur 2017 was a Southeast Asian multi-sport event that took place in Kuala Lumpur,This was the sixth time that Malaysia hosted the games and its first time Since 2001. Previously, it had also hosted the 1965, 1971, 1977 and 1989 editions of the games.

The games were held from 19 to 30 August 2017, although several events had commenced from 14 August 2017. Around 4,646 athletes participated at the event, which featured 404 events in 38 sports. It was opened by the King of Malaysiax, Muhammad V at the Bukit Jalil National Stadium.

By the time the country hosts the game in 2017,it will be 16 years after Malaysia last hosted the biennial event.The Games closing ceremony will coincide with Malaysia's 60th Independence Day.
